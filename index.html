What is World Youth Day?

A pilgrimage of faith, every few years, on a different continent.

World Youth Day (WYD) is the largest gathering of young Catholics in the world. Called by the Pope and lived together with millions of young people from every nation, WYD is a pilgrimage — a walk of faith, prayer, friendship and song that finds its heart in the Eucharist celebrated with the Holy Father.

For the Rwandan youth of the Neocatechumenal Way, each WYD is a step on the road — a chance to discover that the Risen Christ is alive, and that He is calling.
